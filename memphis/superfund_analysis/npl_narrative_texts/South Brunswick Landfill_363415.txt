NPL Site Narrative for South Brunswick
Landfill
 
SOUTH BRUNSWICK LANDFILL
South Brunswick, New Jersey
 
 
Conditions at listing (December 1982) : The South Brunswick Landfill covers 68 acres in a rural
residential area in south Brunswick, New Jersey. The privately-owned site, now closed, consists of two
mounds separated by a small stream that is a tributary of Heathcote Brook. EPA has found that both
ground water and surface water are contaminated with various chemicals, including vinyl chloride, toluene,
benzene, and heavy metals. There are private and public water wells within 3 miles of the site.
 
Status (July 1983) : Under the terms of a Consent Order with EPA, the owner is currently cleaning up the
site.
