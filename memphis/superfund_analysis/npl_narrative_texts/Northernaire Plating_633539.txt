NPL Site Narrative for Northernaire Plating
 
NORTHERNAIRE PLATING
Cadillac, Michigan
 
 
Conditions at listing (July 1982) : The Northernaire Plating Site covers 1 acre in Cadillac, Wexford
County, Michigan. On the site, an inactive electroplating facility, are 2,000 gallons of plating sludge, 2,500
gallons of liquid plating solution in containers, and drums containing wastes contaminated with chrome and
cyanide. A large volume of wastes released through an improperly sealed sewer line has contaminated
ground water and soil. Two private wells are contaminated, and the Cadillac Well Field is threatened by
surface and subsurface releases from the site.
 
Status (July 1983) : EPA recently prepared a Remedial Action Master Plan outlining the investigations
needed to determine the full extent of cleanup required at the site. It will guide further actions at the site.
 
In July 1983, EPA took emergency actions to clean up the site.
