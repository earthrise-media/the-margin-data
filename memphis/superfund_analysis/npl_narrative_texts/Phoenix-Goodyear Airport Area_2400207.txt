NPL Site Narrative for Phoenix-Goodyear
Airport Area
 
PHOENIX-GOODYEAR AIRPORT AREA
Avondale, Goodyear, Arizona
 
 
Conditions at listing (December 1982) : The Litchfield Airport Area Site covers about 4 square miles in
the vicinity of the Phoenix-Litchfield Municipal Airport and the Cities of Goodyear and Avondale, Arizona.
Ground water in the area, which provides drinking water for about 5,000 people, is contaminated with
chlorinated solvents.
 
Status (July 1983) : EPA and the State are cooperating in a investigation to identify the extent, magnitude,
and sources of the contamination. EPA is preparing a Remedial Action Master Plan outlining the
investigations needed to determine the full extent of cleanup required at the site. It will guide further actions
at the site.
