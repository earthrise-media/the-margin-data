NPL Site Narrative for Cliff/Dow Dump
 
CLIFF/DOW DUMP
Marquette, Michigan
 
 
Conditions at listing (December 1982) : The Cliff/Dow Dump is located in Marquette, Marquette County,
Michigan. About 20,000 cubic yards of wood tars, a by-product of the manufacture of charcoal briquets,
were disposed of at the site from 1954 until the late 1960s. Ground water contains benzene, phenol,
xylene, chloroform, and other toxic constituents.
 
Status (July 1983) : Conditions at this site are currently being addressed through an enforcement action
against parties responsible for wastes at the site.
